## Strategic Decision

Phase 1 focused on proving that the AI workflow works.

Phase 2 shifts the focus from building the workflow engine to creating a self-service product that nonprofit organizations can use with minimal setup.

The next objective is to allow an organization to complete a guided onboarding experience that automatically generates a sponsorship initiative and enters the validated workflow engine.
