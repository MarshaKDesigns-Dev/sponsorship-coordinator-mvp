# Sponsorship Coordinator Roadmap

Version: v0.1.0
Status: Phase 1 Complete

---

# Vision

The Sponsorship Coordinator is an AI-powered workflow system that helps nonprofit organizations discover sponsors, manage outreach, track opportunities, and build long-term sponsorship relationships.

The goal is not to create another chatbot.

The goal is to provide an AI employee that executes a complete sponsorship workflow from onboarding through sponsorship fulfillment.

---

# Completed

## Phase 1 — Workflow Engine ✅

The following workflow has been implemented and validated.

- Prospect Research Worker
- Decision-Maker Identification
- Opportunity Approval
- Outreach Draft Generation
- Message Quality Review
- Email Delivery
- Follow-Up Worker
- Follow-Up Regeneration
- Follow-Up Quality Review
- Follow-Up Email Delivery
- Opportunity Pipeline
- Automatic Follow-Up Scheduling
